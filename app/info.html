User Information

Adding a Movie To Either List
1. Using the toolbar, navigate to the list that you would like to add to. Next, select 'Add a Movie'
2. Now, enter the title of the movie that you want information about and press 'Search'
     2.1 For Ranked Movies: add 'Rank' (place in the list), 'Rating' (your personal rating), and 'Times Seen'

Why Do I Keep Getting - "Movie Not Found - Please Try Again'?"
1. Movie titles must be pretty accurate, first make sure that you are typing the name 100% correctly (check the ole google)
2. There may be another movie with the same title from a different year. To fix this problem, type both the movie title and the year it was made, and then press search
3. Ask Alex, maybe he can help. If he can't you're screwed, friend

Other Actions
1. To delete a movie, press the red 'X'
2. To change the rank of a movie, click on its current rank value and modify in the popup
3. To move a movie from the watchlist to the ranked list, press the green icon with the arrow pointing to the right, and then add a rank and personal rating
4. To view a movie trailer on youtube, click the green play button

Sorting a Movie List
On both the 'Favorites' and 'Watchlist' movie lists, by using the search bar in the top right corner and clicking 'Sort by:', you are able to choose the layout of the movies in that list
