Deleting a Movie
Pressing the red 'X' button in the top right corner of a movie block will remove that movie from the list

Sorting a Movie List
On both the 'Favorites' and 'Watchlist' movie lists, by using the search bar in the top right corner and clicking 'Sort by:', you are able to choose the layout of the movies in that list.
Additionally, you can sort by genre by clicking the 'Genre:' button
